### Installation
#### To the fully operational OkÄ™cie Airport you need the EPWA.txt file, which you can find in the same repository, where is this file. After download, drag it to the following path: Endless ATC/locations/
#### Additionally if you want to play in real world traffic density, go to game settings, next Traffic > Custom > flow and set following values:
#### Flow min: 20
#### Flow max: 25

### Project description
#### What have I made:
#### - Runways marking
#### - SIDs and STARs of EPWA and EPMO airport
#### - TMA classes: A, B and C
#### - Possibility to direct to each STAR's waypoint
#### - Real active runways configurations
#### - Real holding courses at entry points
#### - Airport operators
#### - Much more

#### Currently acitve runway avail configurations are:
#### EPWA - 29/33, EPMO - 08
#### EPWA - 29/33, EPMO - 26
#### EPWA - 11/15, EPMO - 08
#### EPWA - 11/15, EPMO - 26
#### SIDs and STARs are fitted to active runways, as in real life.
